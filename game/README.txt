  	    ~~~~GOAL~~~~
  Take down the Tree King's horde of
 minions with your trusty flamethrower.
The horde will get stronger every minute.

  Can you survive 5 minutes and then
   take out the Tree King himself?

   	   ~~~CONTROLS~~~
       Move - Left Stick / WASD
           Attack - X / K
         Pause - Start / Esc
        Cheat Menu - Backspace
